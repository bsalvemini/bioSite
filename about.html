About Deviann

This is Deviann or Devi. She was adopted by our family when she was just a year old. She is now 18 years old. She wears glasses to correct near sightedness. During her junior year of high school Deviann participated in her schools elementary ed program where she worked daily with a class of 2nd graders. She is involved with the Raggedy Ann Revival Effort (RARE) in reviving the lost Raggedy Ann musical called Rag Dolly. Deviann enjoys making people laugh. She believes in ghosts, that rain is the ideal sleeping weather and reincarnation. Deviann is afraid of spiders. Deviann has in interest in urban exploring but would not actively partake in it. Deviann likes maximalism and not minimalism. Devi has a desire to take up cake decorating. Devi has some texture sensitivities that make certain foods unappealing to her. Devi prefers movies over books but does enjoy audiobooks. She has an incredible interest in pop culture and how it affects society negatively and positively. Deviann is interested in Doctor Who, which influenced an interest in time travel in general. Devi does not entirely believe in superstition but does believe in karma. Another interest of Devi's includes true crime, specifically cold cases. Deviann has a strong sense of justice. Deviann has a strong interest in theatre. She has been involved in her school's drama club for the last 1 and a half years. Last year in drama club she was involved in directing and assistant directing and has started acting this year.
