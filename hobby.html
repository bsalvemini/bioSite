Deviann's Hobbies

Deviann has many hobbies. Her hobbies include:

  • Drawing - Character art, abstract art, horror art, fan art
  • Writing - Subjects include romance, horror, fantasy, sci-fi, fan fiction
  • Making bead bracelets
  • Dungeons and Dragons - player, dungeon master, teaching how to play, writing campaigns.
  • Collecting - physical movies, beanie babies, stickers, Star Trek, The Beatles, Raggedy Ann, Ghostbusters, Muppets or Jim Henson products, pins, CDs, Cassettes, clown memorabilia
  • Puppeteering
  • Fashion - Putting together outfits, working with colors and patterns, making things eye-catching or abstract outfits.
  • Cosplay - Spock and Dot Warner from Animaniacs
  • Character analysis - villains (lore from Star Trek), Spock, characters from The Lost Boys
  • Plot analysis - One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest, Grease, Pom Poko, Five Nights at Freddy's
  • Acting
  • Listening to music - Mitski, Penelope Scott, David Bowie, The Beatles, Jack Stauber, Will Wood, Lenoard Nimoy, Steve Martin, “Weird Al” Yankovic, Oingo Boingo, They Might Be Giants, ABBA, Hot Freaks, Muppet music, musical theater
