Welcome to Deviann's bioSite

Deviann's goals include graduating high school, successfully performing in a play, and joining her first puppetry workshop. Deviann's recent accomplishments include directing a show for her school's drama club, winning a free yearbook for her Spock costume in her school's costume contest, and owning her first puppet. She was a hit at a recent murder mystery event at the Roberson Museum when she was mistaken for a cast member. She was personally asked by the director if she would like to be involved in planning and in the cast for the next event. Deviann's interests include Star Trek, The Beatles, The Monkees, movies (especially vintage movies), Dungeons and Dragons, musicals such as Hamilton, and more. She even received a letter back from Lin-Manuel Miranda when she wrote to him. Deviann enjoys talking to her friends on the phone and hanging out with them. Some of Deviann's favorite actors include Gene Wilder, Lenoard Nimoy and Brent Spiner. Some of Deviann's favorite movies include the original Willy Wonka & the Chocolate Factory, Ghostbusters, Blazing Saddles, and Young Frankenstein. Deviann is involved in many clubs at her school, including art club, and drama club. She recently started a Dungeons and Dragons club at her school. Deviann's favorite books include One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest and The Outsiders. Some of Deviann's least favorite actors include Chevy Chase. Some of Deviann's inspirations include Hayao Myazaki, Jim Henson, and Richard Hunt. Her favorite animals include Jellyfish, and Octopi. She is also a fan of mystery and horror. Deviann collects Star Trek, Ghostbusters, Raggedy Ann and other memorabilia.
